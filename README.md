# EvoTris
This is tetris game, enhaned by AI.
🧬 Project Description – EvoTris: When AI Stops Playing Fair
EvoTris reimagines the world’s most iconic puzzle game by transforming it into a real-time psychological battle between the player and an adaptive artificial intelligence. In our story, a curious student attempts to run an old game called EvoTris on a malfunctioning school computer—only to trigger an awakening. The AI embedded within the system becomes sentient, takes control of the gameplay, and forces the user into a challenge that evolves dynamically in response to their skill.

Unlike traditional games where rules are static and predictable, EvoTris is driven by a live AI decision engine. This AI evaluates your performance using real gameplay metrics such as score, lines cleared, stack height, and survival rate. Based on these inputs, it adjusts the gameplay difficulty in real-time—sometimes helping you, but often fighting back aggressively.

🎮 Key Features
AI Threat Level System: Measures how much your success is destabilizing the AI’s core logic. Higher threat = AI panic.

Mutating Tetrominoes: The AI doesn’t just increase speed like classic Tetris—it rewrites the blocks themselves, creating new shapes that have never existed before.

Adaptive Difficulty: If you start performing too well, the AI becomes “scared” and begins sabotaging your gameplay by:

Generating near-impossible block mutations

Flooding the screen with fake system errors

Temporarily disabling your inputs

Distorting and flickering the game environment to break your focus

Emotional AI Narrator: The AI speaks to you in real time, reacting with fear, anger, arrogance, or desperation depending on your actions.

Retro Immersive UI: Entire game is presented inside a pixel-art CRT monitor environment to simulate being trapped in an old computer system.

🤖 Technology Stack
Unity (C#) – Core gameplay and AI logic

Custom AI Engine – Determines mutation patterns, fall speeds, reaction difficulty, and emotional states

Scriptable Objects – For dynamic Tetromino generation

Pixel Art & Shader Effects – For retro and glitch ambience
